  • kondapalli toys manufacturers

    Kondapalli toy manufacturers are artisans from Kondapalli, a town near Vijayawada in Andhra Pradesh, India, famous for creating distinctive and colorful wooden toys known as Kondapalli toys. These toys, made primarily from a special type of softwood called Tella Poniki, showcase rural life, mythology, and local traditions.
